*****************
Weld Tools
*****************
.. note::

 Since Ver 2.2 ``Collapse Tool`` has been renamed to ``Weld Tool`` which are divided into 3 tools. ``Weld To First``, ``Weld To Ave`` and ``Weld to Last``.
