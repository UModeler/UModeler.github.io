*******************************************************************************************************************
Boundary Alignment Tools |Icon_UVEditor_Alignment_AlignBoundary|
*******************************************************************************************************************

Aligns the selected polygons or UV islands with the bound box of the last one.

.. |UModeler_UVEditor_BoundaryAlignment_0| image:: /images/UModeler_UVEditor_BoundaryAlignment_0.jpg
   :scale: 100 %
   
.. |UModeler_UVEditor_BoundaryAlignment_1| image:: /images/UModeler_UVEditor_BoundaryAlignment_1.jpg
   :scale: 100 %
   
|UModeler_UVEditor_BoundaryAlignment_0| |UModeler_UVEditor_BoundaryAlignment_1|
 The number means the order of selection. When you go to ``Align Boundary Tool``, 1st~5th elements will be aligned with the bound box of the 6th element.
