################
Side Stair Tool
################
You can draw a stair profile on a plane.

Interface
------------
``LMB Drag``
 Draws a stair profile
 
``SPACE``
 Confirmation
 
``ESC``
 Cancellation

Properties
------------
Rise
 Distance between two treads.

Flip
 Flips the drawn stair profile.
