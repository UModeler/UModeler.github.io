#####################
Remove Double Tool
#####################

Lets you merge automatically all the selected vertices within the specific distance.

Steps
--------
 1. Select vertices, edges or polygons.
 2. Go to ``Remove Double Tool``
 3. Adjust ``Distance`` property.

Properties
---------------

Distance
 Minimum distance to be merged.
