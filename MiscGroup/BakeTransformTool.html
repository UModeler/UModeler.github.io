Bake Transform Tool¶

Bakes rotate, scale or both of them.

Properties¶

Bake All
Bake both Rotation and Scale.
Bake Rotation
Applys the object rotation to every polygon and sets it to (0,0,0)
Bake Scale
Applys the object scale to every polygon and sets it to (1,1,1)
