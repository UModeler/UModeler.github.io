All/None Selection¶

All Selection - Vertex Edge Polygon ¶

When nothing is selected, all elements will be selected as soon as All Selection button is clicked.

None Selection ¶

This button makes all selected elements unselected so that nothing will be selected.

When you click on button, all UVs will be selected.

When you click on button, all edges will be selected.

When you click on button, all polygons will be selected.
